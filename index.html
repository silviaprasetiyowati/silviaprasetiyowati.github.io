// Research

Personalisation, TikTok & Digital Consumer Shaping

Master's Thesis · Copenhagen Business School · 2024 · Qualitative research with 10 Gen Alpha participants in Denmark

Institution

CBS

Investigated how TikTok's personalisation algorithm impacts Gen Alpha's engagement with advertising in Denmark — combining consumer psychology, behavioral science, and qualitative research methodology. The findings have direct implications for how sustainability brands should approach digital communication: moving beyond awareness campaigns toward genuine information literacy and behavioral change. This research forms the intellectual foundation of how I think about digital sustainability communications and consumer behavior.

Finding 01

Brand and ad relevance relies heavily on ad-attractiveness — not just message content or sustainability claims.

Finding 02

Offline interaction remains essential for meaningful online engagement and genuine trust-building.

Finding 03

Internal motivation is an underexplored but promising factor in personalisation — with real implications for behavior change campaigns.
